[de:Waybar](de:Waybar "wikilink") [pl:Waybar](pl:Waybar "wikilink") [Waybar](https://github.com/Alexays/Waybar) is a
highly customizable [Wayland](Wayland "wikilink") bar for [Sway](Sway "wikilink") and Wlroots based compositors. Its
configuration is done in JSONC with styling in CSS.

## Installation

[Install](Install "wikilink") the `{{Pkg|waybar}}`{=mediawiki} package.

For the icons of the preconfigured modules a font like `{{Pkg|otf-font-awesome}}`{=mediawiki} is needed. Some standard
modules need further programs to function properly. Those are visible through error messages from those modules.
